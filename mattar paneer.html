Mattar Paneer

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup cream or yogurt (optional, for richness)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Aromatics: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  2. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the oil separates from the masala and the mixture thickens.
  3. Add Peas and Paneer: Add green peas and cubed paneer to the pan. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add Water and Simmer: Add about 1/2 cup of water and salt to taste. Cover and cook on low heat until the peas are tender and the flavors blend.
  5. Finish with Cream or Yogurt: (Optional) Stir in cream or yogurt for a richer, creamier texture. Add garam masala and simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve Mattar Paneer hot with naan, roti, or rice.
