Masala Dosa

Ingredients

For Dosa batter

  • 1 cup rice (preferably dosa rice or parboiled rice)
  • ¼ cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 tablespoons chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • ¼ teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for soaking and grinding)

For The Masala (Potato Filling)

  • 3 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

Preparing The Dosa Batter

  1. Soak Ingredients Soak rice, urad dal, chana dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind and Ferment: Grind into a smooth batter, add salt, and let it ferment overnight.

Potato Masala

  1. Tempering: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal, and curry leaves.
  2. Cook Filling: Add onions, green chili, turmeric, and salt, then add boiled, mashed potatoes. Mix well and set aside.

Making Masala Dosa

  1. Cook Dosa: Pour batter on a hot tawa, spread thin, and cook until golden.
  2. Add Filling: Place potato masala in the center and fold.
  3. Serve: Enjoy with coconut chutney and sambar.
