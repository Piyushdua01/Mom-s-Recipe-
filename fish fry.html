Fish Fry

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (such as tilapia, cod, or pomfret)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2-3 tbsp rice flour or cornflour (for a crispy coating)
  • Oil for shallow frying
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Fish: In a bowl, mix turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and lemon juice. Rub the mixture over the fish fillets and let marinate for at least 15-30 minutes.
  2. Coat the Fish: Lightly coat the marinated fish fillets in rice flour or cornflour for extra crispiness.
  3. Fry the Fish: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Place the fish fillets in the pan and shallow fry until golden brown and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes on each side, depending on thickness.
  4. Drain and Garnish: Remove the fish from the pan and place on paper towels to drain excess oil. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  5. Serve: Serve Fish Fry hot with lemon wedges and onion slices, or as a side dish with rice or roti.
