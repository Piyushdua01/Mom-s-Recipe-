Prawn Masala

Ingredients

  • 500g prawns, cleaned and deveined
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Prawns: In a bowl, mix turmeric and salt, then add the prawns and toss well. Let marinate for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Cook the Onions: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add Ginger-Garlic Paste: Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Cook until the tomatoes soften and oil begins to separate from the masala.
  5. Add Prawns: Add the marinated prawns to the pan. Stir well and cook for 5-7 minutes until prawns turn pink and are cooked through.
  6. Finish with Garam Masala: Sprinkle garam masala and mix well. Cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  7. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve Prawn Masala hot with rice, naan, or roti. Serve with lemon wedges if desired.
