Jalebi

Ingredients

  • For the Batter:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Water as needed
  • Ghee or oil for frying
  • For the Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp saffron (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Batter: In a bowl, combine flour, cornflour, and baking soda. Add yogurt and enough water to make a thick, smooth batter. Cover and let it ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight.
  2. Make the Sugar Syrup: In a saucepan, combine sugar, water, saffron, and cardamom powder. Boil until slightly thickened, then add lemon juice. Keep warm.
  3. Fry the Jalebis: Pour the batter into a squeeze bottle or piping bag. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and pipe spirals of batter into the hot oil. Fry until golden and crispy.
  4. Soak in Syrup: Remove the fried jalebis and immediately dip them into the warm sugar syrup for a few seconds. Remove and let excess syrup drip off.
  5. Serve: Serve Jalebi warm or at room temperature, garnished with a sprinkle of saffron if desired.
