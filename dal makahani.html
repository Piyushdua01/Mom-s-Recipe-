Dal Makahani

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Black lentils(Urad Dal)
  • 1/4 Cup Kidney beans(rajma)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 2 Large tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chillies
  • 1 tbsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp Red Chilly Powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 coriander powder
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • Fresh Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Soak & Cook: Soak lentils and beans overnight. Drain, then pressure cook with 4 cups water and salt for 6-7 whistles until soft.
  2. Prepare Base: In a pan, heat 2 tbsp butter, add cumin, onions, ginger-garlic paste, and chilies. Cook until golden. Add tomato puree and spices, cooking until oil separates.
  3. Combine: Add cooked dal to the base with water as needed. Simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Finish: Stir in 2 tbsp butter, 3 tbsp cream, and garam masala. Simmer 10 more minutes.
  5. Garnish: Top with cilantro and extra cream if desired. Serve hot!
