Kheer

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup basmati rice, rinsed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4-5 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 2-3 tbsp chopped almonds and cashews
  • 2-3 tbsp raisins
  • A few strands of saffron (optional)
  • 1 tsp rose water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the Milk: In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, bring the milk to a boil over medium heat. Stir occasionally to prevent it from burning.
  2. Add Rice: Add the soaked and drained rice to the boiling milk. Stir well, and let it cook on low heat until the rice softens, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Add Sugar and Flavoring: Once the rice is cooked, add sugar and crushed cardamom pods. Stir well to dissolve the sugar.
  4. Add Nuts and Saffron: Add the chopped almonds, cashews, raisins, and saffron strands. Cook for another 10-15 minutes until the kheer thickens to your liking.
  5. Finish with Rose Water: (Optional) Add rose water just before turning off the heat for a fragrant finish.
  6. Serve: Serve the kheer warm or chilled, garnished with extra nuts or saffron, as desired.
