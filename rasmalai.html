Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • For the Rasgullas:
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • For the Milk Syrup:
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4-5 cardamom pods, crushed
  • A few strands of saffron (optional)
  • 2-3 tbsp chopped almonds and pistachios
  • 1 tsp rose water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make the Chhena (Paneer) for Rasgullas: Boil the milk and add lemon juice to curdle it. Strain through a muslin cloth, rinse with water, and hang to remove excess moisture. Knead the chhena until smooth.
  2. Shape and Cook Rasgullas: Divide chhena into small balls. Boil water and sugar to make a syrup, then add the chhena balls. Cook for 15 minutes until they expand, then let them cool.
  3. Prepare the Milk Syrup: Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan, stirring until it reduces to half. Add sugar, crushed cardamom, saffron, and chopped nuts. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  4. Combine and Chill: Gently squeeze the cooled rasgullas to remove excess syrup and add them to the milk syrup. Let them soak for 2-3 hours in the refrigerator.
  5. Serve: Serve Rasmalai chilled, garnished with more nuts and saffron if desired.
